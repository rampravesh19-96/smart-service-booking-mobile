# Privacy Policy for Smart Service Booking

Smart Service Booking is a demo portfolio application that showcases a service and appointment booking experience.

## Data handled by the app

The app may allow users to enter demo information such as name, phone number, address, booking details, and profile information for the purpose of demonstrating booking flows.

The app may use mock API data and test payment flows. It does not provide real medical consultation, emergency services, or real appointment fulfillment.

## Payments

The app may include Razorpay test payment integration for demonstration purposes only. No real payments are collected through the demo app.

## Data sharing

This app does not sell user data or share user data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.
